4. Description of Service

Clique Pix is a private, event-based photo sharing application designed for sharing photos within trusted groups. The core functionality of the Service includes:

Circles. Circles are persistent groups that you create or join. Each Circle represents a group of people (such as friends, family, or teammates) with whom you wish to share photos. You can invite others to join your Circles using invite links, text messages, or QR codes.

Events. Events are temporary photo sharing sessions created within a Circle. When you create an Event, you select a duration: 24 hours, 3 days, or 7 days. All photos uploaded to the Event are automatically and permanently deleted from our cloud storage when the Event expires.

Photo sharing. Within an active Event, Circle members can take photos using the in-app camera or upload photos from their device's gallery. Photos are compressed and stripped of metadata on the device before upload. Uploaded photos appear in the Event feed and are visible only to members of the associated Circle.

Reactions and saving. Circle members can react to photos using a set of lightweight reactions. Members can also save individual photos to their personal device. Photos saved to your device remain on your device and are not affected by the automatic cloud deletion that occurs when an Event expires.

Automatic deletion. When an Event's duration expires, all associated photos are automatically and permanently deleted from our cloud storage. This is a core feature of the Service, not a bug. Photos that you have saved to your device before expiration are not affected.

We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any aspect of the Service at any time, with or without notice. We will make reasonable efforts to provide advance notice of significant changes that materially affect the Service.
